==============
Tips of Sloper
==============

1. Observe sloper and find the best location to hold.
2. Hand formation, use bundle fingers(4 fingers stay together) for non sphare, use seperated fingers for sphare.
3. Lower shoulder with 90 degreee of elbow. Fully stright arm above shoulder is ok but isn't easy to move.
4. To acquire stable, push waist towards wall aka put your body underneath the sloper. However, to jump to next hold, right under the sloper isn't a good
   place to launch. You have to control your waist come out from wall first and then jump/launch.
